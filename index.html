In recent years, many college students have wanted to help with efforts, whether it be COVID-19 donations, hurricane relief efforts, disease efforts, or helping with food drives. Still, most people have no idea where to start. We at Volunteer U have created a user-friendly website to make it easier to find and support relief efforts near U! In this day and age, where everything is virtual, there are many opportunities for scammers to take advantage of your kindness through fake charities or person-in-need scams. Volunteer U serves as a middleman to connect students directly to service opportunities in their communities.
